Our Story

A mission born from
real life.

Two founders. Two countries. One conviction — that no child should have their future determined by where they were born.

🇧🇪🇨🇩
Emmanuel Naweji — CEO & Co-Founder

Born in Belgium and raised in Kolwezi, DR Congo, Emmanuel lost his father shortly after graduating high school — closing the door to higher education through no fault of his own. An American woman and her family — a stranger who became family — funded his studies in Zimbabwe, then in Evanston, Illinois. That act of generosity became the foundation of everything he builds. From Sioux Falls, SD, he co-built EMLink with Jean Gilbert, driven by one conviction: the same opportunity that changed his life should be available to every student in Congo.

🇨🇩
Jean Gilbert Mbula Mboma — CTO & Co-Founder

Jean Gilbert built his technical expertise from within the DRC — a system with almost no formal infrastructure — which is precisely what makes him exceptional. As CTO, he leads all platform architecture and engineering from Kinshasa, combining deep technical skill with an intimate, ground-level understanding of the DRC education system and the students EMLink was built to serve.

"

The opportunity to pursue higher education — funded by who was once a stranger, now family — drives me to invest in education. I do not want any kid left behind when it comes to leveraging AI to succeed at school, life, and work, while competing with any youth from around the world.

Emmanuel Naweji

What We've Built

The EMLink
AI Ed Platform.

Emmanuel and Jean Gilbert co-built EMLink together — Education Platform, AI Chat, and Employment Portal already live and operational. Mentorship program on Cohort 4K. Student AI is the next focused layer on top of what already works.

Module 01
Exam Prep AI
High School · Examen d'État

Aligned to the DRC Ministry of Education (EPST) curriculum. Generates French-language practice questions, explains concepts step by step, tracks weak areas, and adapts to each student's level automatically.

Module 02
University Assistant
University · UNIKIN · UPC

Helps university students write research papers, structure arguments, prepare for oral and written exams, and format citations — filling the gap left by campuses with no writing centers or tutoring.

Module 03
Career Path Advisor
Graduation · What Comes Next

When a student doesn't know where to go after school, EMLink guides them. AI maps their strengths to university programs, scholarships, and career paths — in French, culturally relevant, locally grounded.

Module 04
Résumé Builder & Job Coach
Career · Local + Global

AI-crafted résumés and cover letters in French and English — tailored for DRC employers, NGOs (UN, World Bank), and diaspora job markets. Interview prep included. Connected to EMLink's Employment Portal.

Business Model

Sustainable by design.
Purposeful by nature.
