## Overview

This is the official mission landing page for **EMLink** — a global digital infrastructure platform connecting lifestyle, business, and enterprise across borders, rooted in the African diaspora community.

The page tells the story of EMLink's AI Education Platform, built for students in the DR Congo — starting with the Examen d'État and expanding to university, career, and employment support.

> *"No kid left behind. Not in Kolwezi. Not in Kinshasa. Not anywhere."*


Search for `emmanuelnaweji.com` to update Emmanuel's personal website link in the Founders section.
